## Mistral AI Powered Assistent Guide

### Which Model

Le Chat can use Mistral Large or Mistral Small under the hood, or a prototype model called Mistral Next, designed to be brief and concise. These models are part of Mistral AI's conversational assistant, which is designed to enhance both personal and professional life by providing access to the latest news, planning daily activities, tracking projects, uploading and summarizing documents, and more. Le Chat is available on iOS, Android, and will soon be available on private infrastructure for enterprises12.
