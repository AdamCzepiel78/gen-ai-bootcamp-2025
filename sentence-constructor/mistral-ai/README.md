## Mistral AI Powered Assistent Guide

### Which Model

Le Chat can use Mistral Large or Mistral Small under the hood, or a prototype model called Mistral Next, designed to be brief and concise. These models are part of Mistral AI's conversational assistant, which is designed to enhance both personal and professional life by providing access to the latest news, planning daily activities, tracking projects, uploading and summarizing documents, and more. Le Chat is available on iOS, Android, and will soon be available on private infrastructure for enterprises12.

### Prompting Guides

When you first start using Mistral models, your initial interactions will revolve around prompts. Crafting effective prompts is crucial for generating desirable responses from Mistral models or other LLMs. Here are some key points to consider when prompting Mistral AI models:

Instruction Fine-Tuned Models: Mistral AI's models, such as the 7B and 8x7B Instruct variants, are designed to understand and follow instructions. These models have been specifically trained to perform tasks based on the prompts provided3.

Prompt Construction: A well-constructed prompt can significantly improve the quality of the responses generated by the model. For example, using special tokens like <s>, [INST], and [/INST] can help guide the model's output. These tokens indicate the beginning of a sequence, the instruction, and the end of the instruction, respectively3.

Role-Playing: Providing the model with a specific role, such as "You are a bank customer service bot," can add personal context and often leads to better performance. This technique helps the model understand the context and generate more relevant responses4.

Few-Shot Learning: Including a few examples in the prompt can help the model generate corresponding outputs based on the demonstrations provided. This technique is known as few-shot learning or in-context learning4.

Example Generation: Asking the model to generate examples with explanations and steps can guide the reasoning and understanding process. This technique helps the model provide more structured and informative responses4.
